Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Fudge

Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Fudge is a creamy, indulgent treat made with
dairy-free chocolate, offering sweet and salty flavors.

How To:

Prep Time: 10 - 15 Minutes Cooking Time: 0 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6

Ingredients

  1. coconut butter
  2. peanut butter
  3. bananas
  4. cocoa powder
  5. agave nectar
  6. vanilla extract

Instructions

Step One

Combine all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.

Step Two

Fold mixture into a flat, sealable container and freeze for 1 hour or refrigerate overnight.

Step Three

To make your own coconut butter, pulse shredded coconut in a food processor until smooth; about 8-12 minutes.
